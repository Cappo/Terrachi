After traversing across the lands and overcoming many obstacles, the young kodama disrupts the Grey’s facility, halting both its production and the resulting waste. The kodama makes it through to the fertile land. The journey, however, has taken its toll. The young kodama uses its last remaining energy to move forward and plant the sapling. Its mission complete and its life force depleted, the young kodama happily accepts its fate and begins to fade...
